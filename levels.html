Selecting a level

Below you will find rough descriptions of the levels available at Ljubljana Bunny Hop 2025. To ensure the best possible experience for yourself and other attendees, please be fair when choosing your level.

Intermediate

You are comfortable with one or multiple basic steps and leading/following basic moves to reasonably fast music on the social dance floor.
Recommended 6-12 months of attending collegiate shag classes.

Intermediate-advanced

You are comfortable with a sizable amount of basic steps, moves and breaks, even when dancing to faster music on the social dance floor. Your focus is shifting from individual moves towards dancing as a whole.
Recommended 1-2 years of attending collegiate shag classes.

Advanced

You are comfortable with a sizable amount of basic steps, moves and breaks. Your focus is beyond those, you are looking to improve your technique, connection, and improvisation. The song tempo is just a number.
Recommended 2+ years of attending collegiate shag classes.

About the Organizers

The event is organized by passionate individuals under the umbrella of Studio Dansa, a swing dance studio in Ljubljana. Founded in 2009, it offers classes in all major swing genres, and regularly organizes swing dance parties, weekend workshops, and festivals.
