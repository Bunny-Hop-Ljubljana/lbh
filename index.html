The Bunny Hop Experience

In spring, we invite you to Ljubljana, a beautiful city with a thriving swing and a growing collegiate shag scene, for a cozy weekend with two days of workshops and parties!

Expect inspiring workshops with two top teaching couples and a like-minded shag community. To top it off, get ready to dance at two evening swing dance parties, including one with live music.

Registration opens October 1st 2025 - save the date and check back soon!
