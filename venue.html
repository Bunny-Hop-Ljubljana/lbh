Ljubljana Bunny Hop 2026

Venue

Both the classes and the parties will be held at Studio Dansa, located on Vilharjeva cesta 3.

The location is easily accessible via the Ljubljana city bus LPP, with the "Razstavišče" bus stop being just a five minute walk away.
Alternatively, it is within ten minutes walking distance from two BicikeLJ city bicycle rental stations ("LIDL Bežigrad" and "Vilharjeva").
It does not have its own (public) parking lot, but there are many of those nearby - some even offer free parking during weekdays after 19:00, and during the weekend.

More information:
https://www.lpp.si/en (LPP bus service)
https://www.bicikelj.si/en/home (BicikeLJ city bicycle rental service)
