Registration terms & conditions

In the following days after your registration you can expect an e-mail from us with payment instructions (please also check your spam folder). Solo registrations will be put on a waiting list and processed when they can be matched with a complementary solo registration.
Conditions: Please make your payment via bank transfer in the 7 days after our confirmation e-mail. If we do not receive your payment in the 7 days after our confirmation, your registration will be cancelled.
In case you need to cancel your registration after payment:
- if you cancel by January 7, we return 70% of your payment;
- if you cancel by February 7, we return 30% of your payment;
- if you cancel after February 7 2025, we give no refunds, nor can you use your payment for our future events; you can however sell or give away your ticket to somebody else (same level and lead/follow role as your pass unless otherwise agreed with the organizer).
In case we, the organizer, cancel the event, we return your payment in full. We are not however responsible for any other costs (accommodation, flights etc.) you might endure.
If you have any questions, contact us at info@studiodansa.com.

About the Organizers

The event is organized by passionate individuals under the umbrella of Studio Dansa, a swing dance studio in Ljubljana. Founded in 2009, it offers classes in all major swing genres, and regularly organizes swing dance parties, weekend workshops, and festivals.
